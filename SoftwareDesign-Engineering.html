Software Design & Engineering Enhancement

This page showcases my enhanced artifact in the area of Software Design and Engineering. It highlights my understanding of modular architecture, object-oriented principles, and modern development practices.

Project Overview

The artifact selected for this category is the OpenGL 3D Scene Renderer, originally developed in CS-330: Computational Graphics and Visualization. The application uses C++, OpenGL, GLFW, and GLM to render a 3D environment featuring lighting, textures, and camera navigation.

Enhancements Made

For this enhancement, I added a real-time frames-per-second (FPS) counter to monitor rendering performance using std::chrono. I also refactored code to better separate responsibilities across classes such as ViewManager, SceneManager, and ShaderManager. In addition, I improved lighting and shading consistency, documented all major classes and functions, and implemented error checking for shader compilation.
